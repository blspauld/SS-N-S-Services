About Us

Named for the Sivori family, Sean, Sheri, Nicolas, and Samantha; Sean Sivori, owner and operator of SS-N-S Services, started the business in 2005. He had been working as a master electrician for 10 year and had begun doing cabinetry on the weekends as a side job. Sean's attention to detail and high quality of finished work led to a steady increase in business as his reputation continued to grow. After a few years the business had grown so productive, not to mention profitable, that Sean decided to quit his day job and focus 100% on his business.

Being a certified electrician, owning CDL certification, and generally being a very adaptable and creative person Sean was quickly able to branch the business out to other areas. Hauling, electrical work, industrial maintenance, and general contracting there is little that SS-N-S Services can't handle. Sean's son Nicolas now works with his father learning the trade. His wife, Sheri, works in the office and takes care of all estimates, invoices, and billing.
